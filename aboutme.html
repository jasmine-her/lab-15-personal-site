Currently, I am a Senior Graphic Designer at a Commercial Real Estate firm, looking to pivot into Front-End Development. Coding fascinates me. Although i have taken a few courses and bootcamps, I am still trying to find my footing in the digital design space. I attended the High School of Art and Design in the 90's and went on to earn my BA in Communication Design at our very own NYC College of Technology in early 2000. From time to time, I mentor junior designs at my old high school and other organizations that are involved with inspiring young art enthusiast. Typography, and Art History are some of my favorite subjects. This quote from Walter Groupius has always inspired me, along with the Bauhaus movement (also DaDA). Aside from graphic designer and aspiring Front-End Developer, I am also an urban gardener, advid reader and fur mom to my 7 year blue nose pitty. He's name is Gunner ♥
